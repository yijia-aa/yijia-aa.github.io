2019

Joining Indian Dance and vying for the vice president is really a difficult choice for me.

As a Chinese, I had never read about Indian culture or watched anything about Indian dance - but dancing has its charm, the charm of being boundless, inclusive, and attractive to any audience. This is why I love to dance.

I danced for 4 years in the same club, and I saw many old people left and new people joined. During the 4 years, I gradually learnt their culture, language, and dance costumes. I danced pieces of work that revered Hindu god Hanuman, respected the divine rive Ganga, honoured the power of all Moms...

We practised many genres of dance, not only classical and bollywood dances in Indian dance, but western dances like Hip Hop and Jazz. It formed part of my most cherished experience in National Junior College.

We are National Junior College Indiance Dance, NJID, Not Just Individual Dancers!
