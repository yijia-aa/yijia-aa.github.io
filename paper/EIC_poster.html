PROTOTYPE
After various rounds of secondary research on different materials used, we created
our prototype, which is a plastic phone case enhanced with 3 additional layers and
two external antenna.
Graphs 3 (left) and 4 (right): Speedtest of the effectiveness of the three layers combined in a phone
case, in terms of downloading speed and uploading speed respectively.
In Graphs 3 and 4 shown above, we combined all three layers and conducted several
rounds of speed tests. From the graphs, the results reflect very stable and noticeable
improvements of the WiFi speed — an average increase of 9.8 Mbps for downloading speed
and 14.9 Mbps for uploading speed. In addition, this result is valid at all ranges of original WiFi
speed, from as low as 1 to as high as 90, which proves our prototype a success.
