25 December 2020

It is the first Christmas that my friends and I celebrated, though it is our 4th year in Singapore. For various reasons like studies or returning to our home country, we did not celebrate Christmas together, even once.

2020 is a special... specially bad year. Everyone seems to wish 2020 to pass as soon as possible. Most of us believe that 2021 will be a whole new start, a bright year, whilst some are more pessimistic, and they believe that the misfortunes will continue, or exacerbate, in 2021. Will 2021 be another catastrophic year? I hold an optimistic view.

Anyways, not the time for me to worry about these things beyong my control... My 2021 will be I struggling with university admission preparations...

Ah... go back to the 2020 Christmas! We had roasted fish at Tanyu@313summerset for dinner, and we went to Gardens by the Bay where all Supertrees were lit!
