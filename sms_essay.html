2019 & 2020

I participated in Singapore Mathematics Society (SMS) Essay Competition for two consecutive years. I didn't make to any top award winner, but the learning experience is valuable to me.

Each year, SMS requires us to consider how mathematics is used in another area. In 2019, it was Economics, and in 2020, it was artificial intelligence.

Without guidance from any teacher, I had to pick a topic and read into the topic on my own. It indeed challenged me in looking for relevant materials to read and understand what they are talking about, especially when a topic is totally new to me - like in 2019, before I started to learn economics.

I am proud that I managed to finish the essay, which put a completion mark to my self-investigative learning process.
