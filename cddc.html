June ~ July 2020

Before I signed up for the camp, I self-learnt linux basics in May.

In June, I saw the invitation to join Cyber Defence Discovery Camp (CDDC). It involved some knowledge about linux, so I joined. For practical reasons, I would like to test my learning outcomes.

It was my first experience of hacking. We formed a team of four, and all of us went through a one-month training before the actual contest. We learnt about how computers work, cyber security, practical cryptography, linux and windows operating systems, and most excitingly, hacking the computer.

The event is called "capture the flag" (CTF), because every "flag," or a secret message, was hidden in a file and we were supposed to decode the file to obtain the secret lines. To capture the flags, all skills taught during the training were required.

I had a good time in the contest. All members in my team were very excited and active in decoding the files. A fun experience for everyone!~
