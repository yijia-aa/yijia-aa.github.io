September 2019 ~ September 2021

National Junior College Boarding School is my second home. I stayed in this place for four years since I came to Singapore in 2018.

I took up the leadership position in my second year. I was the overall student in-charge of all emails sent to our boarders, communication between teachers and students, students' Value-In-Action activities, and a few one-off events like Chinese New Year dumpling making and the Go Green recycling programme.

Leading a team of 4 to 5 was not as easy as I thought, especially some members were my seniors and some were my juniors. I had to schedule every meeting at least one week in advance to make sure all everyone was free. Mis-communication was the red line, because our commitee held the utmost important role in delivering messages and comminucate with teachers, as well as third-party organisations.

This experience was tough but fruitful. I learnt to write emails tactfully, to communicate with politeness and clarity.
